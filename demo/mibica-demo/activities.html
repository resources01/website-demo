Ayun Pa Harvest 2026: MIBICA Leadership Ensures Superior Tobacco Quality at the Source

Date: March 22nd, 2026

Location: Ayun Pa, Gia Lai Province

At MIBICA, we believe that global trust is built on the ground, not just behind a desk. As an independent leader in the industrial commodities sector, we are committed to full transparency and rigorous quality control at every stage of the supply chain.

On March 22nd, 2026, MIBICA’s Board of Directors conducted a high-level field mission to the Ayun Pa region in Gia Lai province—one of the premier tobacco cultivation hubs in Vietnam—to oversee the peak of the 2026 harvest season.

This visit allowed our leadership to engage directly with the specialized farms, kiln operators, and processing facilities within the MIBICA network. By maintaining this direct presence in the fields of Gia Lai and Dak Lak, we ensure that the raw materials we deliver to our international partners meet the most exacting industrial standards.

Exceptional Results: The 2026 Burley Crop

The 2026 harvest is proving to be a landmark season. Our team’s on-site inspections and preliminary factory tests have yielded exceptional data for this year’s Burley tobacco output:

• Visual Excellence: The leaves exhibit a consistent, vibrant golden-yellow hue, a hallmark of superior curing.
• Chemical Profile: Laboratory analysis confirms high concentrations of both Nicotine and Sugar, ideal for premium industrial applications.

These results are a direct outcome of the sophisticated cultivation techniques practiced across our growing regions. From the uniformity of the large-growth leaves to the optimized post-harvest processes—including standardized drying, meticulous grading, and precision threshing—MIBICA ensures that every batch represents the pinnacle of Vietnamese tobacco production.

Unmatched Stability for Global Supply Chains

For international partners seeking a reliable and scalable source of tobacco raw materials, MIBICA offers proven stability.

Following extensive consultations with regional stakeholders and processing plants, we estimate that the Ayun Pa region alone will produce 50,000 tons of threshed, dried tobacco this season. This significant volume underscores our capacity to fulfill high-demand contracts while maintaining the consistent quality that modern manufacturing requires.
