MIBICA Tobacco Portfolio – Key Tobacco Varieties We Supply

• Burley Tobacco (Our Core Specialty)
Air-cured Burley tobacco is known for its low sugar content, rich body, and cocoa-like nutty aroma. Its neutral yet full-bodied profile makes it an essential base component in many balanced tobacco blends.

• Virginia (Flue-Cured) Tobacco
Virginia tobacco is recognized for its bright yellow to orange leaf color and naturally higher sugar content. It produces a mild burn with a smooth, slightly sweet aroma, making it a popular choice for blends requiring balance and smoothness.

• Oriental Tobacco
Oriental tobacco features small leaves with an intensely aromatic profile. Traditionally sun-cured, these leaves contribute a distinctive fragrance and complexity, playing an important role in premium cigarette blends.
