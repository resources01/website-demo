MIBICA: Your Transparent Partner in Vietnamese Natural Rubber

At MIBICA, transparency is the foundation of a reliable global supply chain. We specialize in sourcing and exporting premium Vietnamese natural rubber, including SVR 10, SVR 3L, and RSS 3, connecting Vietnam’s leading rubber-producing regions with international manufacturers.

Our role goes beyond simple trading. MIBICA provides a transparent sourcing network that allows our partners to understand exactly where their rubber comes from, how it is processed, and how it reaches their production facilities.

Our Commitment to Transparency

What sets MIBICA apart is our dedication to providing complete visibility throughout the supply chain. We offer our partners detailed information on every stage of sourcing, processing, and delivery.

• Strategic Sourcing Regions: We procure natural rubber from Vietnam’s key rubber-growing areas, including Binh Phuoc, Dong Nai, and the Central Highlands, regions known for stable supply and high-quality latex production.

• Direct Manufacturing Partnerships: MIBICA collaborates with certified Vietnamese rubber processing factories, ensuring consistent quality and clear origin verification for every shipment.

• Full Supply Chain Traceability: Our documentation system tracks the journey of each batch from plantation to processing facility and final export, supporting compliance with international regulations such as EUDR.

• Strict Product Specifications: All rubber products strictly comply with TCVN 3769:2016 standards, and every shipment is accompanied by detailed laboratory reports and quality documentation.

Our Natural Rubber Product Portfolio

MIBICA supplies premium Vietnamese natural rubber sourced from certified processing facilities across Vietnam. Our portfolio focuses on the most widely used industrial grades, ensuring consistent quality and reliable performance for global manufacturers.

1. SVR 3L (Standard Vietnamese Rubber 3L)

Produced from fresh field latex, SVR 3L is a high-purity block rubber known for its light color, excellent elasticity, and consistent quality. It is widely used in industries requiring clean and flexible rubber materials.

Common Applications
* Shoe soles and footwear components
* Medical rubber products
* Automotive parts
* Bright-colored technical rubber products

Transparent Standards (TCVN 3769:2016):
* Dirt Content: Max 0.03%
* Ash Content: Max 0.5%
* Initial Plasticity (Po): Min 35
* Plasticity Retention Index (PRI): Min 60

2. SVR 10 (Standard Vietnamese Rubber 10)

SVR 10 is a technically specified rubber (TSR) produced from field coagulum. It provides a strong balance of durability, resilience, and cost efficiency, making it suitable for heavy-duty industrial applications.

Common Applications
* Tire manufacturing
* Conveyor belts
* Industrial rubber components
* Rubber mats and molded products

Transparent Standards (TCVN 3769:2016):
* Dirt Content: Max 0.08%
* Ash Content: Max 0.6%
* Initial Plasticity (Po): Min 33
* Plasticity Retention Index (PRI): Min 50

3. RSS 3 (Ribbed Smoked Sheet 3)

RSS 3 is a globally recognized grade of ribbed smoked sheet natural rubber, produced through controlled coagulation and traditional smoke-drying processes. This method enhances durability while preserving the natural properties of rubber.

Common Applications
* Heavy-duty tires (truck, aircraft, off-road vehicles)
* High-performance technical rubber components
* Industrial rubber manufacturing
